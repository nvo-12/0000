Welcome to Otto's

This spoiled house dog loves attention and whines for everything; food, cuddles, or being let on the couch. His dramatic eyes and soft whimpers make him irresistible, so his family can't help but pamper him despite his crybaby ways.

Otto, now 12, has learned to play with a ball and follow simple commands like sit and lie down, showing his growth and adaptability.
